Leadership Excellence

Executive Leadership at EGML

Yusuf Sheriff Modu, Chief Executive Officer of Explore Global Market Limited, holds a Master of Business Administration (MBA) and is a trained computer and information systems professional. He began his career at Sterling Bank Plc, Maiduguri Branch, Borno State, where he served as Head of Marketing and Retail Banking and was recognized as Best Performing Staff in the North-East Region on three occasions.

His leadership blends financial acumen, technological insight, and deep governance expertise to drive EGML's mission of sustainable, inclusive growth. He is a Fellow of multiple prestigious institutions including the Chartered Institute of Administration (FCIA), Chartered Institute of Leadership and Governance (FCILG), Chartered Institute of Public Diplomacy and Management (FCIPDM), and Institute of Corporate Administration of Nigeria (FICAD).

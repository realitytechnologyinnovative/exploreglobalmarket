About Explore Global Market Limited

Bridging International Capital with African Opportunities

Explore Global Market Limited (EGML) is a multidisciplinary company established with a forward-looking vision to operate across diverse sectors of the global economy. Since its incorporation, the company has been strategically positioned to deliver integrated solutions spanning investment and financial services, technology and digital solutions, general trading and supplies, construction and infrastructure, renewable energy, and foreign investment facilitation.

At Explore Global Market Limited, our mission is to enable businesses, institutions, and investors by delivering structured, innovative, and value-driven solutions across multiple sectors. We support our clients and partners in making informed decisions, optimising resources, and unlocking sustainable growth through integrated financial, technological, trading, infrastructure, and investment-focused services.

Our Founder & CEO

Yusuf Sheriff Modu - Chief Executive Officer

Yusuf Sheriff Modu holds a Master of Business Administration and is a trained computer and information systems professional. He began his professional career in the banking sector with Sterling Bank Plc in Maiduguri, Borno State, where he served as Head of Marketing and Retail Banking. During his tenure, he distinguished himself through exceptional performance and was recognised three times as the Best Performing Staff in the North-East region.

He is a Fellow of several respected professional institutions, including the Chartered Institute of Administration, the Chartered Institute of Leadership and Governance, the Chartered Institute of Public Diplomacy and Management, and the Institute of Corporate Administration of Nigeria. His professional development is further strengthened by advanced training in diplomatic practice for organisational and national economic sustainability, legislative processes and economic recession management, strategic implementation of development models during economic downturns, and the principles and practice of professional administration, with a strong focus on corporate leadership and governance.

These academic, professional, and executive experiences collectively inform his strategic leadership style, strong governance orientation, and commitment to sustainable business growth.
